Electric Vehicle Registrations by Province — Spain (DGT Microdata)

Data source: DGT (Dirección General de Tráfico) — MATRABA Monthly Vehicle Registration Microdata
Choropleth map showing cumulative EV registrations (pure electric + plug-in hybrid) by Spanish province, derived from DGT monthly microdata files. Darker shading indicates higher total EV stock. A second layer shows annual registration growth rate, highlighting provinces with the fastest adoption. This distribution directly informs where future public charging demand will be highest. Hover over each province for exact registration counts and propulsion type breakdown.
